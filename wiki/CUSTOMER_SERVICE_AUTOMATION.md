# Customer Service Automation

Automating customer support and service workflows.

---

## 💬 Service Tasks

**Ticket analysis**: Categorize, prioritize issues
**Response generation**: Create helpful replies
**FAQ generation**: Document common issues
**Knowledge base**: Self-service resources
**Escalation**: Route complex issues
**Satisfaction tracking**: Monitor happiness

---

## 🔄 Support Pipeline

```
Customer inquiry → Analysis → Response → Resolution → Feedback
       ↓            ↓         ↓          ↓           ↓
Email      Categorize   Generate    Execute      Collect
Chat       Extract      Template    Document     Improve
Social     Sentiment    Personalize Track
```

---

## 🤖 Agent Capabilities

- **Issue classification**: What's the problem?
- **Response generation**: Helpful answer
- **FAQ creation**: Prevent future issues
- **Escalation routing**: To right team
- **Knowledge linking**: Relevant articles
- **Quality checking**: Helpful response?

---

## 📊 Metrics

- Response time
- Resolution rate
- First-contact resolution
- Customer satisfaction (CSAT)
- Net promoter score (NPS)
- Ticket volume trends
