# Predictive Maintenance

Predicting equipment failures before they happen.

---

## 🔮 Maintenance Types

**Reactive**: Fix when broken (expensive)
**Preventive**: Fix on schedule (wasteful)
**Predictive**: Fix before failure (optimal)
**Prescriptive**: Recommended actions

---

## 📊 Data Collection

**Sensors**: Temperature, vibration, pressure
**Usage Logs**: Operating hours, stress
**History**: Past failures and repairs
**Context**: Environmental factors

---

## 🔄 Prediction Process

```
Historical data
     ↓
Train failure prediction model
     ↓
Monitor real-time sensor data
     ↓
Detect anomalies/risk patterns
     ↓
Alert for maintenance
     ↓
Schedule service
```

---

## 📈 Failure Indicators

**Vibration**: Increasing frequency
**Temperature**: Rising unexpectedly
**Performance**: Degrading output
**Error Rate**: Increasing failures
**Usage Pattern**: Unusual behavior

---

## 💡 Benefits

- **Reduced Downtime**: Plan maintenance
- **Cost Savings**: Fix before failure
- **Safety**: Prevent catastrophic failure
- **Efficiency**: Optimal service timing
- **Inventory**: Right spare parts ready

---

## 🎯 Success Metrics

- **Mean Time Between Failures (MTBF)**
- **Prediction Accuracy**: True positive rate
- **False Alarm Rate**: Cost of unnecessary maintenance
- **Maintenance Cost**: Savings achieved
- **System Availability**: % uptime
