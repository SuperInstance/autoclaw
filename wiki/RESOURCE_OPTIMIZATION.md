# Resource Optimization Techniques

Maximizing efficiency and minimizing waste.

---

## 🎯 Optimization Targets

**Compute**: CPU cycles
**Memory**: RAM usage
**Network**: Bandwidth
**Storage**: Disk space
**Time**: Response latency
**Cost**: Dollar spending

---

## 🔧 Optimization Techniques

**Algorithmic**: Better algorithms
- O(n²) → O(n log n)
- Better data structures
- Reduced operations

**Caching**: Avoid recomputation
- In-memory cache
- Distributed cache
- Cache invalidation

**Batching**: Process together
- Amortize overhead
- Reduce context switches
- Network efficiency

---

## 📊 Profiling & Identification

```
Identify bottleneck
    ↓
Measure impact
    ↓
Test optimization
    ↓
Measure improvement
    ↓
Deploy
```

---

## ⚖️ Trade-offs

**Latency vs Throughput**:
- Fast response OR many responses
- Often conflict

**Quality vs Speed**:
- Accurate vs fast
- Can trade for performance

**Resource vs Complexity**:
- Simple but slow
- Complex but fast
