# Data Governance & Quality

Managing data as an organizational asset.

---

## 📋 Governance Components

**Data Ownership**: Who's responsible?
**Data Quality**: Standards and measures
**Access Control**: Who can access?
**Retention**: How long kept?
**Lineage**: Where did it come from?

---

## 📊 Data Quality Dimensions

**Accuracy**: Correct values
**Completeness**: No missing values
**Consistency**: Uniform format
**Timeliness**: Current and fresh
**Validity**: Meets constraints

---

## 🔄 Quality Assurance

```
Raw data
    ↓
Validation rules
    ↓
Quality checks
    ↓
Cleansing
    ↓
Storage
```

---

## 📑 Data Catalog

**Inventory**: What data exists?
**Metadata**: Describe each dataset
**Lineage**: Data flow tracking
**Quality**: Known issues
**Access**: Who can use?

---

## 🔐 Privacy & Compliance

- Data Classification: Public, sensitive, restricted
- Access Logging: Who accessed what?
- Retention Policies: How long to keep?
- Deletion Procedures: How to purge?
- Audit Trail: Complete history
