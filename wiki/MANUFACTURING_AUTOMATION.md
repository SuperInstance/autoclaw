# Manufacturing & Production Automation

Automating manufacturing and production workflows.

---

## 🏭 Manufacturing Tasks

**Quality Control**: Defect detection
**Production Planning**: Scheduling and optimization
**Supply Chain**: Logistics and inventory
**Predictive Maintenance**: Prevent failures
**Process Optimization**: Efficiency improvement

---

## 🔄 Production Pipeline

```
Raw materials → Production → Quality check → Packaging → Distribution
     ↓             ↓             ↓              ↓            ↓
Plan         Schedule      Detect          Ship        Track
Track        Monitor       Rate-limit       Optimize    Analyze
```

## 🤖 Automation Applications

**Visual Inspection**: Computer vision for defects
**Predictive Maintenance**: When to service
**Demand Forecasting**: Predict orders
**Route Optimization**: Shipping efficiency
**Inventory Planning**: Right stock levels
