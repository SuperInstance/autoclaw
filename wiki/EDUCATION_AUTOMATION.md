# Education & Learning Automation

Automating educational content and course management.

---

## 🎓 Education Tasks

**Course design**: Curriculum structure, flow
**Content generation**: Lessons, materials
**Quiz generation**: Test creation, grading
**Assignment grading**: Evaluation, feedback
**Progress tracking**: Student advancement
**Adaptive learning**: Personalized paths

---

## 📚 Content Generation

**Lecture materials**: Slides, transcripts
**Exercises**: Practice problems, solutions
**Quizzes**: Assessment questions, answers
**Case studies**: Real-world examples
**Videos**: Script generation, captions
**Discussions**: Forum prompts, moderation

## 🔄 Learning Pipeline

```
Curriculum → Content → Delivery → Assessment → Feedback → Adaptation
    ↓          ↓          ↓         ↓           ↓          ↓
Outline    Generate   Organize   Create      Evaluate   Personalize
Learning   Materials  Platform   Tests       Provide     Next steps
Outcomes   Examples   Structure  Grading     Comments
```
