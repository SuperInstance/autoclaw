# Federated Knowledge & Learning

Building shared knowledge across distributed systems.

---

## 🌐 Federated Architecture

```
Organization A → Local knowledge → Federated sync → Organization B
     ↓                ↓              ↓               ↓
Knowledge         Store &         Share &         Learn &
creation          Learn           Aggregate        Apply
```

---

## 🔄 Knowledge Sharing Models

**Centralized**: Single source of truth
**Federated**: Distributed with sync
**P2P**: Direct peer sharing
**Hybrid**: Mix of above

---

## 🔒 Privacy & Sharing

- De-identification
- Selective sharing
- Access controls
- Audit trails
- Conflict resolution
- Consensus building

---

## 📊 Synchronization

- **Push**: Share new knowledge
- **Pull**: Request knowledge
- **Event-based**: Share on update
- **Scheduled**: Periodic sync
- **Conflict**: How to merge disagreements

## 💡 Use Cases

- Multi-organization research
- Distributed teams
- Industry consortiums
- Open knowledge networks
- Collaborative learning
