# Innovation Pipeline & Research Workflows

Managing innovation and experimental workflows.

---

## 🔄 Innovation Stages

```
Ideation
    ↓
Prototyping
    ↓
Experimentation
    ↓
Validation
    ↓
Deployment
    ↓
Monitoring
```

---

## 💡 Idea Management

**Collection**: Capture all ideas
**Evaluation**: Score by impact × effort
**Prioritization**: Focus on high-impact
**Planning**: Resource allocation
**Execution**: Team assignment

---

## 🧪 Experimentation

**Hypothesis**: What we believe
**Design**: How to test
**Execution**: Run experiment
**Analysis**: Measure results
**Decision**: Ship or learn?

---

## 📊 Metrics for Innovation

- **Innovation Rate**: New ideas/quarter
- **Success Rate**: % shipped ideas
- **Time-to-Market**: Idea to deployment
- **Impact**: User metric improvement
- **Learning**: Knowledge gained

---

## 🎯 Portfolio Management

**Maintain**: Keep existing working
**Improve**: Incremental enhancement
**Explore**: New opportunities (high risk/reward)

Allocation:
- 70% Maintain
- 20% Improve
- 10% Explore
