# Quality & Performance Metrics

Measuring system quality and performance characteristics.

---

## 📊 Key Metrics

| Metric | Target | Warning | Critical |
|--------|--------|---------|----------|
| Task completion rate | >95% | <95% | <90% |
| Avg confidence | >0.8 | <0.8 | <0.7 |
| Knowledge accuracy | >95% | <95% | <90% |
| Response time | <5s | >5s | >10s |
| Error rate | <5% | >5% | >10% |
