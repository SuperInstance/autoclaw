# Real Estate Automation

Automating real estate workflows and analysis.

---

## 🏠 Real Estate Tasks

**Property analysis**: Valuation, comparables
**Listing generation**: Create compelling descriptions
**Market research**: Area analysis, trends
**Lead scoring**: Buyer/seller qualification
**Document generation**: Contracts, disclosures
**Investment analysis**: ROI, cash flow

---

## 💰 Analysis Types

**Valuation**: Estimate property value
**Market analysis**: Comparable sales, trends
**Investment analysis**: Cap rate, cash flow
**Risk assessment**: Market risk, legal
**Demographic analysis**: Area characteristics
**Trend analysis**: Price movements, development

---

## 📋 Document Generation

- **Listings**: Property descriptions, photos
- **Contracts**: Purchase agreements, terms
- **Disclosures**: Legal requirements
- **Reports**: Market, property analysis
- **Marketing**: Email, social, websites

---

## 📊 Metrics

- Days on market
- Price per square foot
- Offer to list ratio
- Closing success rate
- ROI on investments
- Market absorption rate
