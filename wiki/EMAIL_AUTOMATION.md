# Email Campaign Automation

Automating email generation, personalization, and optimization.

---

## 📧 Email Tasks

**Content generation**: Create compelling copy
**Personalization**: Customize per recipient
**Segmentation**: Target right audiences
**A/B testing**: Optimize performance
**Send timing**: When to send
**Performance tracking**: Measure success

---

## 🔄 Email Pipeline

```
Audience data → Segmentation → Content → Personalization → Send
      ↓            ↓           ↓           ↓              ↓
Users      Identify groups  Generate  Add names        Track
Behavior   Preferences      Templates  Dynamic          Analytics
History    Demographics     Subject    Content
```

---

## 💡 Content Generation

- **Subject lines**: Attention-grabbing
- **Body copy**: Engaging, clear
- **Call-to-action**: Compelling asks
- **Templates**: Reusable structure
- **Variables**: Personalization fields
- **Images**: Visual enhancement

---

## 📊 Optimization

- **Subject line testing**: What gets opens?
- **Copy testing**: What drives clicks?
- **Timing testing**: When to send?
- **Segment testing**: Which version works best?
- **Frequency testing**: How often?
