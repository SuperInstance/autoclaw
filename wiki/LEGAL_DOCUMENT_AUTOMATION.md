# Legal Document Automation

Automating legal document processing and analysis.

---

## ⚖️ Legal Tasks

**Contract analysis**: Extract terms, identify risks
**Case research**: Find relevant precedents
**Document generation**: Create contracts
**Compliance checking**: Regulatory requirements
**Due diligence**: Comprehensive review
**Risk assessment**: Legal exposure analysis

---

## 📄 Document Pipeline

```
Raw documents → Analysis → Extraction → Structure → Review
     ↓             ↓          ↓          ↓         ↓
Contracts     Parse terms  Obligations Generate  Validate
Cases         Identify     Rights       Format    Flag risks
Statutes      Key clauses  Deadlines    Link      Summarize
```

---

## 🔍 Analysis Capabilities

- **Obligation extraction**: What must be done?
- **Risk identification**: Potential issues
- **Compliance checking**: Legal requirements
- **Precedent finding**: Similar cases
- **Clause comparison**: Standard vs. custom
