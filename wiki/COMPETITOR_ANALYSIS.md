# Competitive Intelligence Automation

Automating competitor analysis and market intelligence.

---

## 🏢 Competition Monitoring

**Product analysis**: Features, pricing, roadmap
**Marketing analysis**: Campaigns, messaging, channels
**Financial analysis**: Revenue, growth, investment
**Tech stack analysis**: Tools, platforms, capabilities
**Team analysis**: Talent, expertise, growth
**Partnership analysis**: Integrations, alliances

---

## 📊 Analysis Pipeline

```
Competitor data → Collection → Analysis → Intelligence → Distribution
       ↓              ↓           ↓          ↓             ↓
Websites        Scrape        Extract    Synthesize     Reports
Social          Normalize     Patterns   Compare        Alerts
News            Validate      Trends     Benchmark      Insights
```

---

## 🔍 Data Sources

- Company websites
- Social media
- News articles
- Patent databases
- Job postings
- Press releases
- Financial reports

---

## 📈 Analysis Dimensions

- **Strengths**: What they do well
- **Weaknesses**: Where they struggle
- **Opportunities**: Market gaps
- **Threats**: Competitive moves
- **Positioning**: Market differentiation
- **Strategy**: Business direction
