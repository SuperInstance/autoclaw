# Compliance & Audit

Meeting regulatory requirements and maintaining audit trails.

---

## 📋 Audit Requirements

- **Traceability**: Who did what, when, why
- **Immutability**: Logs cannot be modified
- **Retention**: Keep logs for required period
- **Reporting**: Audit reports available
- **Access control**: Who can view logs

---

## 🔐 Data Compliance

- **Privacy**: PII handling (GDPR, CCPA)
- **Encryption**: Data protection standards
- **Retention**: Data deletion policies
- **Backup**: Disaster recovery planning
- **Segregation**: Data isolation

---

## 📝 Documentation Requirements

- API documentation
- Architecture diagrams
- Operational runbooks
- Change logs
- Incident reports
