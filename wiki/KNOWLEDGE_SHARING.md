# Knowledge Sharing & Collaboration

Sharing knowledge across teams and organizations.

---

## 📚 Knowledge Export Formats

- **JSON**: Machine-readable, structured
- **Markdown**: Human-readable, documentation
- **CSV**: Spreadsheet-compatible
- **PDF**: Distribution format
- **HTML**: Web publishing

---

## 🔄 Knowledge Sync

- **Merge conflicts**: Handle disagreements
- **Version control**: Track changes
- **Approval workflows**: Review before publishing
- **Distribution**: Push to stakeholders

---

## 👥 Collaboration Models

**Centralized**: One master knowledge base
**Federated**: Multiple local bases, sync periodically
**Hybrid**: Mix of above

---

## 💡 Best Practices

- Clear ownership
- Version control
- Approval process
- Documentation standards
- Regular sync/updates
