# Incident Management & Response

Managing and responding to system incidents.

---

## 🚨 Incident Lifecycle

```
Detection
    ↓
Triage
    ↓
Containment
    ↓
Remediation
    ↓
Recovery
    ↓
Post-mortem
```

---

## 📋 Severity Levels

**Critical (P1)**: Complete outage, revenue impact
**High (P2)**: Major degradation, some users affected
**Medium (P3)**: Minor degradation, workaround available
**Low (P4)**: Cosmetic, no impact on users

---

## 🔍 Detection

**Automated**: Monitoring alerts
**User Reports**: Customer complaints
**Internal**: Team notices issues
**Proactive**: Security scans

---

## 🛠️ Response Process

1. **Acknowledge**: Confirm incident
2. **Assess**: Severity and scope
3. **Assign**: Add responder
4. **Communicate**: Keep stakeholders updated
5. **Investigate**: Root cause analysis
6. **Fix**: Implement solution
7. **Verify**: Confirm resolution
8. **Publish**: Status page update

---

## 📊 Metrics

- **MTTR**: Mean Time To Resolve
- **MTTA**: Mean Time To Acknowledge
- **Availability**: % uptime
- **Incident Rate**: # incidents per month
- **False Alert Rate**: # false alarms

---

## 📝 Post-Mortem

- What happened?
- Why did it happen?
- What was the impact?
- How to prevent?
- Action items?
