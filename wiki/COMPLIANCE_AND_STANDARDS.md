# Compliance & Industry Standards

Meeting regulatory and industry requirements.

---

## 📋 Regulatory Frameworks

**GDPR** (EU): Data protection and privacy
**CCPA** (California): Consumer privacy rights
**HIPAA** (Healthcare): Health information privacy
**PCI-DSS** (Finance): Payment card security
**SOC 2** (Cloud): Security and availability

---

## ✅ Compliance Checklist

- [ ] Data inventory and classification
- [ ] Privacy policy updated
- [ ] Data retention policy defined
- [ ] Access controls implemented
- [ ] Audit logging enabled
- [ ] Breach notification procedures
- [ ] Data deletion procedures
- [ ] Vendor assessments
- [ ] Regular audits scheduled
- [ ] Staff training completed

---

## 🔐 Key Requirements

**Consent**: User permission for data usage
**Transparency**: Clear about what we do
**Control**: User can access/delete data
**Security**: Data protected from breaches
**Accountability**: Responsible for data

---

## 📊 Documentation

- **Privacy Policy**: Public statement
- **Data Processing Agreement**: Legal contract
- **Audit Trail**: System of records
- **Risk Assessment**: Potential issues
- **Incident Response**: Breach procedures
