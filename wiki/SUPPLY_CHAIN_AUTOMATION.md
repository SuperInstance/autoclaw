# Supply Chain Automation

Automating supply chain management and optimization.

---

## 📦 Supply Chain Tasks

**Demand forecasting**: Predict future needs
**Inventory optimization**: Right stock levels
**Supplier analysis**: Evaluate partners
**Logistics planning**: Route optimization
**Risk management**: Identify disruptions
**Performance tracking**: Monitor efficiency

---

## 🔄 Supply Chain Pipeline

```
Demand data → Forecast → Plan → Execute → Monitor → Optimize
    ↓           ↓        ↓       ↓        ↓         ↓
Sales      Predict    Order   Ship    Track     Improve
Trends     Scenarios  Routes  Deliver Quality   Efficiency
Seasonality Buffer    Partners Check   Issues
```

---

## 📊 Analysis Capabilities

- **Demand forecasting**: Time series, ML models
- **Inventory optimization**: ABC analysis, EOQ
- **Supplier evaluation**: Cost, quality, reliability
- **Route optimization**: Shortest path, cost
- **Risk assessment**: Single points of failure
- **Performance metrics**: On-time, quality

---

## 💡 Optimization

- Reduce lead times
- Lower inventory costs
- Improve fill rates
- Minimize waste
- Optimize routes
- Strengthen suppliers
