# Sustainability & Impact Metrics

Measuring environmental and social impact.

---

## 🌍 Impact Dimensions

**Environmental**: Carbon, water, energy
**Social**: Job creation, accessibility, equity
**Economic**: Cost efficiency, value creation
**Ethical**: Alignment with values

---

## 📊 Carbon Metrics

**Carbon Intensity**: kg CO2 per task
**Embodied Carbon**: Manufacturing impact
**Operational Carbon**: Running impact
**Offset**: Carbon credits purchased

---

## 📈 Measurement Framework

**Baseline**: Current state
**Target**: Desired state
**Progress**: Weekly/monthly tracking
**Attribution**: What drove improvements?

---

## 🎯 Improvement Strategies

1. **Right-sizing**: Use minimal compute needed
2. **Efficiency**: Optimize algorithms
3. **Renewable**: Use green energy providers
4. **Offsets**: Carbon credits
5. **Education**: Team awareness

---

## 📋 Reporting

- Carbon per API call
- Carbon per trained model
- Energy sources used
- Efficiency trends
- Offset purchases
