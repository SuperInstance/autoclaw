# Knowledge Graph Construction

Building semantic knowledge graphs for complex domains.

---

## 🔗 Graph Basics

```
Entities (nouns)
    ↓
Relationships (verbs)
    ↓
Knowledge Graph
    ↓
Queryable structure
```

---

## 🏗️ Construction Process

1. **Extract entities** from documents/code
2. **Identify relationships** between entities
3. **Validate consistency** across sources
4. **Resolve duplicates** with entity linking
5. **Store in graph** database
6. **Query and analyze** patterns

---

## 💡 Entity Types

- Concepts
- People
- Organizations
- Places
- Technologies
- Processes

---

## 🔗 Relationship Types

- `is_a` (inheritance)
- `part_of` (composition)
- `related_to` (general)
- `contradicts` (conflict)
- `extends` (building on)
- `references` (citation)
