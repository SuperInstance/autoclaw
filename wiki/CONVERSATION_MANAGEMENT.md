# Conversation & Context Management

Managing long-running conversations and context preservation.

---

## 💬 Context Management

**State tracking**: What's been discussed
**Memory management**: Important details
**Context switching**: Multiple conversations
**Thread management**: Conversation trees
**Handoff**: Pass to human or other agent
**Recovery**: Resume interrupted conversations

---

## 🔄 Conversation Flow

```
Initiate → Context gathering → Processing → Resolution → Wrap-up
   ↓            ↓                ↓            ↓          ↓
Open        Understand          Execute      Confirm    Document
Greeting    Details             Actions      Success    Followup
```

---

## 🧠 Memory Systems

**Short-term**: Current conversation
**Long-term**: Historical information
**Episodic**: Specific past events
**Semantic**: General knowledge
**Procedural**: How to do things

---

## 🔄 Context Handoff

- Preserve conversation history
- Transfer understanding
- Continue seamlessly
- Maintain relationship
- Document resolution
- Plan followup
