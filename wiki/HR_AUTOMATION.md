# Human Resources Automation

Automating HR processes and talent management.

---

## 👥 HR Tasks

**Candidate screening**: Evaluate resumes, applications
**Job posting generation**: Create descriptions
**Interview scheduling**: Coordinate meetings
**Assessment generation**: Create evaluations
**Onboarding**: New hire documentation
**Performance tracking**: Goal management

---

## 🔄 HR Pipeline

```
Job opening → Recruiting → Screening → Interviews → Hiring → Onboarding
    ↓           ↓            ↓          ↓           ↓         ↓
Write        Post         Filter      Schedule    Offer     Train
Process      Promote      Score       Coordinate  Admin     Support
Analyze      Collect      Rank        Feedback    Setup     Track
```

---

## 📋 Automation Capabilities

- **Job description**: Auto-generate with skills
- **Candidate screening**: Assess fit based on criteria
- **Interview generation**: Create relevant questions
- **Assessment creation**: Skill evaluations
- **Onboarding docs**: Employee handbook, policies
- **Performance reports**: Synthesis of feedback

---

## 📊 Metrics

- Time to hire
- Candidate quality
- Retention rate
- Employee satisfaction
- Training effectiveness
- Hiring ROI
